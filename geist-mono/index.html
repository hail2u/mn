Geist Mono

by Andrés Briganti, Mateo Zaragoza, Guillermo Rauch, Evil Rabbit, José Rago, Facundo Santana

Geist Mono is a monospace typeface that prioritizes readability and seamless integration into coding environments. It is complemented by its sans-serif counterpart, Geist.

Created by Vercel in collaboration with Basement Studio, Geist fonts embody their design principles of simplicity, minimalism, and speed, drawing inspiration from the renowned Swiss design movement. With precision, clarity, and functionality at its core, Geist enhances the visual experience of developers and designers, empowering them to effectively communicate their ideas.

To contribute, see github.com/vercel/geist-font.
